Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

How can I apply for admission?

You can visit our admissions office, complete the application form, and submit the required documents. See the Admissions page for detailed requirements.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based, need-based, and skills-based scholarships. Contact the admissions office for more details.

What programs do you offer?

We offer 9+ vocational programs including Automotive Technology, Welding, Electrical Installation, IT, Hospitality, Business Studies, and more.

Do you provide student accommodation?

Yes, we have hostel facilities available for students. Accommodation fees are optional and separate from tuition.

What is the program duration?

Most programs are 3 years long, with some specialized programs being 2-3 years. Check the Programs page for specifics.
