Admissions

Start your journey to a successful vocational career

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission to St Joseph's Vocational Secondary School, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Completed primary education or equivalent (O-Level/Secondary 2)
  • Valid identification document (National ID, Passport, or Birth Certificate)
  • Original certificates and academic transcripts
  • Good health and fitness for practical work
  • Ability to pay school fees (scholarships available for deserving students)
  • Age: Minimum 16 years at the time of enrollment
  • Strong commitment to practical learning
  • Clear conduct/character reference

Application Process

  1. Application Form: Complete the application form available at the admission office or online
  2. Submit Documents: Provide all required documents and recent passport photo
  3. Application Fee: Pay a non-refundable application fee of 50,000 UGX
  4. Interview: Attend a brief interview to assess aptitude and motivation
  5. Admission Decision: Receive admission decision within 2 weeks
  6. Registration: Complete registration and fee payment to secure your place
  7. Orientation: Attend school orientation program

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We believe quality education should be accessible. We offer several scholarship options:

  • Merit Scholarships: For students with excellent academic records
  • Need-Based Scholarships: For financially disadvantaged students
  • Skills-Based Scholarships: For students demonstrating exceptional practical abilities
  • Community Scholarships: For deserving community members
  • Employer-Sponsored Programs: Partnership with industries for skilled worker training

Students interested in scholarships should contact the admissions office for application details.
