About Our School

St Joseph's Vocational Secondary School is committed to providing quality vocational education that prepares students for successful careers and entrepreneurship. Located in Nyamityobora, our institution offers a wide range of practical and theoretical training programs designed to meet industry standards.

Mission

To empower students with practical skills, knowledge, and values that enable them to contribute meaningfully to society.

Vision

To be a leading vocational institution recognized for producing skilled, ethical, and entrepreneurial graduates.

Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Service to Community.
