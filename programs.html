Our Programs

Choose from a variety of industry-relevant vocational courses

Automotive Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Description: This program equips students with comprehensive skills in vehicle maintenance, repair, and servicing.

Course Content:

  • Engine maintenance and repair
  • Transmission and drivetrain systems
  • Electrical and ignition systems
  • Brake and suspension systems
  • Vehicle diagnostics using modern tools
  • Professional customer service

Career Opportunities: Motor mechanic, Service technician, Fleet manager, Automotive engineer assistant, Self-employment as workshop owner.

Welding & Metal Work

Duration: 3 Years

Description: Learn professional welding techniques and metal fabrication for construction and manufacturing industries.

Course Content:

  • Arc welding (MMAW)
  • Gas welding and cutting
  • Metal fabrication techniques
  • Structural steel work
  • Safety and quality standards
  • Blueprint reading and interpretation

Career Opportunities: Welder, Fabrication specialist, Construction worker, Metal fabricator, Industrial technician.

Electrical Installation

Duration: 3 Years

Description: Master residential and commercial electrical systems installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting.

Course Content:

  • Electrical theory and safety
  • Wiring and cable installation
  • Circuit protection and control
  • Renewable energy systems (solar)
  • Testing and commissioning
  • Electrical regulations and codes

Career Opportunities: Electrician, Electrical maintenance technician, Solar technician, Site supervisor, Electrical consultant.

Hospitality Management

Duration: 3 Years

Description: Develop skills in hotel operations, food service, and customer hospitality management.

Course Content:

  • Hotel and accommodation management
  • Professional food preparation
  • Food service and dining etiquette
  • Beverage service
  • Customer relationship management
  • Hospitality business management

Career Opportunities: Hotel manager, Chef, Restaurant supervisor, Event organizer, Hospitality consultant.

Information Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Description: Build foundational and advanced IT skills for the digital economy.

Course Content:

  • Computer fundamentals and maintenance
  • Operating systems (Windows, Linux)
  • Website design and development
  • Networking basics
  • Database management
  • IT security and ethics

Career Opportunities: IT support technician, Web developer, Network administrator, IT consultant, Software support specialist.

Business Studies & Entrepreneurship

Duration: 3 Years

Description: Prepare for business ownership and management roles with practical entrepreneurship training.

Course Content:

  • Business fundamentals
  • Entrepreneurship and venture creation
  • Accounting and bookkeeping
  • Business law and compliance
  • Marketing and sales
  • Business planning and finance

Career Opportunities: Business owner/entrepreneur, Manager, Accountant, Sales executive, Business consultant.

Construction & Building Technology

Duration: 3 Years

Description: Learn construction skills and building management for the construction industry.

Course Content:

  • Construction safety
  • Building design and layout
  • Masonry and brickwork
  • Carpentry and joinery
  • Plumbing basics
  • Construction project management

Career Opportunities: Construction worker, Building supervisor, Project manager, Contractor, Quantity surveyor.

Plumbing & Water Systems

Duration: 3 Years

Description: Master water supply, sanitation, and plumbing systems installation and maintenance.

Course Content:

  • Water supply systems
  • Sanitation and waste management
  • Pipe work and fittings
  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Water testing and quality
  • Maintenance and repairs

Career Opportunities: Plumber, Water technician, Sanitation specialist, Systems technician, Self-employed plumber.

Hair & Beauty Services

Duration: 2-3 Years

Description: Professional training in hair styling, beauty, and personal grooming services.

Course Content:

  • Hair cutting and styling
  • Hair treatment and care
  • Makeup application
  • Skin care
  • Salon management
  • Beauty entrepreneurship

Career Opportunities: Hairdresser, Beauty specialist, Salon owner, Makeup artist, Beauty consultant.
