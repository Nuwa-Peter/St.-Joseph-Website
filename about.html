About Us

Learn about our school's history, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our History

St Joseph's Vocational Secondary School was established in 2016 with a vision to provide accessible, quality vocational education to students in Nyamityobora and the surrounding regions. Over the years, we have grown from a small institution with modest facilities to a well-equipped vocational training center recognized for excellence in technical education.

Our school is named after Saint Joseph, the patron saint of workers and craftsmen, reflecting our commitment to dignifying labor and equipping students with practical skills for meaningful employment.

Mission Statement

To empower students with practical skills, theoretical knowledge, and values that enable them to:

  • Secure meaningful employment in their fields of study
  • Become self-employed entrepreneurs and job creators
  • Contribute positively to community development
  • Pursue further education and professional advancement
  • Develop as responsible citizens with strong ethical principles

Vision Statement

To be a leading vocational institution in the region, recognized nationally and internationally for producing skilled, ethical, entrepreneurial graduates who drive innovation and sustainable development.

Core Values

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in teaching, facilities, and student outcomes.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all endeavors.

Innovation

We embrace modern techniques and continuously improve our programs.

Inclusivity

We welcome students from all backgrounds and provide equal opportunities.

Service

We remain committed to serving our community through quality education.

Respect

We foster a culture of mutual respect among students, staff, and community.

Facilities & Infrastructure

Our campus is equipped with modern learning and training facilities including:

  • Well-equipped workshops for hands-on training
  • Computer labs with current software and hardware
  • Fully stocked library with physical and digital resources
  • Spacious classrooms with modern learning aids
  • Student accommodation facilities
  • Cafeteria serving nutritious meals
  • Sports facilities for student wellness
  • First-aid clinic with trained medical staff

Accreditation & Recognition

Our programs are accredited by the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) and recognized by the Ministry of Education. We adhere to international standards in vocational education and are committed to continuous improvement through regular reviews and stakeholder feedback.
